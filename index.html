Hotel Riu Palace Baja California

Sitting right on El Médano beach in Cabo San Lucas — the only adults-only, all-inclusive palace in Los Cabos. Open bar, four pools, and the ocean right outside your door.

The Riu Palace Baja California sits on the south tip of the Baja California peninsula, right in front of El Médano beach. With over 600 rooms, four outdoor pools (two with swim-up bars), an open bar around the clock, and a full lineup of restaurants — from a show-cooking American breakfast buffet to Japanese and Italian dinners — there is zero reason to leave. The on-site Renova Spa, two tennis courts, and a gym keep you going between rounds. At night, live music, shows, and a nearby nightclub keep the energy up. This is an adults-only, all-inclusive property — exactly what the doctor ordered for a bachelor party in Cabo.
